What it does

  • Uses 5-year daily price data (Yahoo Finance via yfinance).
  • Trains a Ridge Regression model on simple time-series features (momentum, trend, volatility).
  • Shows accuracy on the last 30 trading days:
    • Up/Down Accuracy (% of days direction was predicted correctly)
    • Within X% Accuracy (% of predictions within a chosen error margin)
  • Includes a 10+ company recursive backtest (predictions feed into future predictions).
